About Wealthy Fax International

Wealthy Fax International is a digital service platform focused on online engagement services, user rewards, WhatsApp-based support, and transparent wallet activity for customers across Kenya and beyond.

Established in 2024, our platform has rapidly grown to become a trusted name in the digital economy space. We combine service access, user account management, transaction tracking, and direct WhatsApp support into one clear dashboard experience.

Our mission is simple: "Democratizing digital earnings for every Kenyan." We believe that everyone deserves access to legitimate income opportunities, regardless of their background or education level.

What We Offer

  • ✓ Service Access & Rewards – Track wallet credits, service activity, and referral progress through our verified portal
  • ✓ Instant M-Pesa Withdrawals – Access your earnings quickly and securely directly to your mobile money
  • ✓ Referral Commissions – Build passive income by inviting friends and family to join
  • ✓ WhatsApp Support – Reach our team quickly through WhatsApp for help
  • ✓ Transparent Operations – No hidden fees, clear pricing, and honest communication

Our Values

Integrity: We operate with complete transparency and honesty in all our dealings.
Innovation: We continuously improve our platform to provide the best user experience.
Inclusivity: We welcome everyone – from students to professionals, urban to rural residents.
Reliability: When you earn with us, you can count on timely payments every time.

Join thousands of Kenyans who are already transforming their financial lives with Wealthy Fax International. Whether you're looking for a side hustle or a primary income source, we're here to help you succeed in the digital economy.
